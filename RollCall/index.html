HOW TO PLAY

How to Play

🎲 Roll the dice to reveal a category and letter count.

🏃 Race to shout a word that fits the category and contains exactly that many letters. You only get one guess per round, so make it count!

⭐ Win the round to earn points equal to the dice total.

🧠 Doubles trigger special memory rounds and increase the point multiplier. Consecutive doubles can lead to huge point swings!

🏆 First player to 100 points wins!
